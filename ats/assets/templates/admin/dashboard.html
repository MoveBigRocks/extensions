Applicant Tracking

This extension turns shared Move Big Rocks primitives into a focused hiring workspace: vacancies, candidate intake, application stages, and recruiter workflows all stay on the same operational base as the rest of the business.

Vacancy model

  • Open, paused, closed, and archived vacancy states
  • Published careers paths per role
  • Dedicated application form and queue mapping

Candidate intake

  • Public application flow creates candidate cases
  • Applicant profiles stay linked to contacts and attachments
  • ATS automation tags new applications for triage

Operator actions

  • Publish or close vacancies
  • Move applicants through screening, interview, offer, and outcome stages
  • Keep hiring operations in the same workspace model as the rest of Move Big Rocks
